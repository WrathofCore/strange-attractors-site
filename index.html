The system

One hash. One attractor. Forever.

At mint, the contract stamps your token a unique hash drawn from on-chain data. That hash seeds a deterministic renderer. Feed it the same hash and it draws the exact same piece on any machine, for as long as the chain exists. Nothing is pre-rendered. The image is computed live from the seed every time it loads.

Why it matters

The renderer lives in the contract.

No IPFS. No server. No image files sitting on someone's cloud. The script, the traits, and the animation are stored on-chain, so every piece regenerates from just a browser and Robinhood Chain.
